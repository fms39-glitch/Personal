Xyris Vision — Privacy-First Image Redaction

Sharing photos online exposes GPS coordinates, device metadata, and identifiable faces without users realizing it. Xyris Vision processes images entirely in-browser or in-memory with zero server-side persistence, automatically scrubbing metadata and redacting PII.

Built in 20 hours at NJx Hackathon 2026 — reached 50+ active users during the event. Live at xyrisvision.vercel.app.

Clinical Ontology Evaluation — LLMs as Ontology Servers

Can large language models reliably serve as ontology servers for clinical concepts? This research framework benchmarks LLMs against 400+ SNOMED CT concepts, evaluating accuracy and consistency in healthcare knowledge representation.

Collaborators: PhD student Naren Khatwani and faculty mentor Dr. James Geller at NJIT, Newark, NJ.

Agentic AI & MCP Integration for Business Workflows

Exploring how Model Context Protocol (MCP) and agentic architectures can automate repetitive business tasks — from job application pipelines to data analysis workflows — by connecting LLMs to real tools and APIs in production environments.

Focus areas: RAG pipelines, automated workflow integration, backend automation, and GenAI-powered decision support.
